Date Place
December 23rd Lakeside Pokhara

On the occasion of the English New Year 2022, the 23rd Pokhara Street Festival will be organized from 15th to 17th Poush (30th December to 1st January 2022). The festival is being organized by Restaurant and Bar Association Pokhara (REBAN) with the objective of promoting Pokhara as an 'English New Year's destination'.
